PRIZES

  • credit does not apply to any third-party services that might be required

Only one (1) entry per household per contest. Winners are not eligible to participate in any Bonfire Labs or Phoenix Editorial Services, Inc. contest or sweepstakes for a period of one hundred and eighty days (180) from the date of qualifying for or winning a contest or sweepstakes conducted or sponsored by Bonfire Labs or Phoenix Editorial Services, Inc.
